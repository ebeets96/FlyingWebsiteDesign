EAA Young Eagles

If you are between the ages of 8 and 17, you are in an incredible opportunity to take advantage of EAA's Young Eagles Program.

The Young Eagles is a program designed to make kids interested in aviation. To do this, they have thousands of pilots all over the world who volunteer their time and airplanes to give free airplane rides to kids. Not only does the program offer free airplane rides to kids, but they also have many additional offers after recieving a flight such as free online ground school and a flight lesson voucher.

To recieve more information about the Young Eagles program and all of the information it has to offer, visit their website here.
