What You Need to Know

Cost

Learning to fly can be an expensive venture. Depending on your location, you can be look at a price between $6000- $12,000.

When looking into flight training, shop around for both low prices, experience flight instructors, and quality of their fleet. These factors, as well as your committment to training, will affect the final price of your training.

Requirements

In the United States, the requirements to get your pilot's license are minimal. To get started, you will simply need a proof of birth to be approved by the TSA. Other requirements are as follows:

  • 16 years of age to solo
  • 3rd class medical to solo
  • 17 year of age to get certificate
  • Government issued ID

Instructors

Choosing an instructor is an important process. It is important to find someone who is compatible to your personality type.

To see more information about choosing the right instructor, visit this website.


EAA Young Eagles

If you are between the ages of 8 and 17, you are in an incredible opportunity to take advantage of EAA's Young Eagles Program.

The Young Eagles is a program designed to make kids interested in aviation. To do this, they have thousands of pilots all over the world who volunteer their time and airplanes to give free airplane rides to kids. Not only does the program offer free airplane rides to kids, but they also have many additional offers after recieving a flight such as free online ground school and a flight lesson voucher.

To recieve more information about the Young Eagles program and all of the information it has to offer, visit their website here.
